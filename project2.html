The core concept of this project is to make a functional online library, termed “E-Library”, where a group of users henceforth named “Readers” can access books in an online platform much like how one can access physical books from a normal library. There will be two other categories of users in our project, “Publishers” and “Admin”. The “Publisher” can, publish books and edit book descriptions while the “Admin” can do the aforementioned as well a few more things like, deleting books, searching for certain users or transactions etc.
